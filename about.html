About Power Haul International Ltd

Supplying genuine diesel engines and dependable service to businesses across Nigeria.

Company Overview

Who we are

Power Haul International Ltd supplies and sources industrial, marine, truck and generator diesel engines, along with the spare parts businesses need to keep them running. We work with construction firms, transport operators, manufacturers, marine operators and power-generation businesses across Nigeria.

Our Story

How we got here

Power Haul International Ltd was founded to close a real gap in the Nigerian market: too many businesses were losing time and money to unreliable, unverified engines. We set out to be a supplier operators could trust — genuine units, straight answers, and support that continues after the sale.
